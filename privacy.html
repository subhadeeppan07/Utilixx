Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: March 2026

🔒 Short version: Utilixx is a free tool website. Most tools work entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.

📋 1. Information We Collect

Utilixx is designed to respect your privacy. Here is what we collect and don't collect:

  • We do NOT collect: Names, emails, passwords, or personal details unless you contact us.
  • We do NOT store: Any text, files, or data you enter into our tools. All processing happens in your browser.
  • We may automatically collect: Basic analytics data such as page views, browser type, country, and device type through Google Analytics or similar services.

🍪 2. Cookies

Utilixx may use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies: To understand how visitors use our site (via Google Analytics).
  • Advertising cookies: If Google AdSense ads are displayed, Google may use cookies to show relevant ads.
  • Preference cookies: To remember your settings and preferences on the site.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

📢 3. Advertising

Utilixx uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to show ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it to serve ads based on your visit to our site.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
  • We do not control Google's data collection practices.

🔧 4. How Our Tools Work

All tools on Utilixx process your data directly in your browser:

  • Text tools, calculators, converters — run 100% in your browser. No data is sent to our servers.
  • QR Code Generator — sends your text to a third-party API (api.qrserver.com) to generate the QR image.
  • Currency Converter — fetches live rates from open.er-api.com. No personal data is shared.
  • IP Lookup — uses ipapi.co to retrieve location data for the IP you enter.
  • Grammar Checker — uses LanguageTool's public API. Your text is sent to their servers temporarily.
  • AI Tools — if you use AI tools, your text is sent to Anthropic's API using your own API key. We never see or store your API key or the content you submit.

🔗 5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

👶 6. Children's Privacy

Utilixx is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

🔄 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

📬 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
